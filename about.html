About GREEN BACK restaurant

we believe hospitality is more than just a service — it’s a warm invitation to experience the heart and soul of Rwanda. Situated in the lively city of Musanze, we combine modern comfort with authentic Rwandan charm to offer you a unique dining and lodging experience. Whether you’re here to enjoy our carefully crafted local cuisine, celebrate a special moment, or simply relax in one of our cozy rooms, our mission is to make you feel right at home. With attentive service, delicious meals inspired by local flavors, and a welcoming atmosphere, GREEN BACK Restaurant&lodges is more than just a place to eat or stay — it’s a destination for creating lasting memories.
